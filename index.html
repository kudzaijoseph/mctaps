Mc Taps

Tapiwa Kwiripi porpularly know as Mc Taps, is an Afropop, Hip-Hop artist and entrepreneur based in Midlands, Kwekwe. He is the founder of GoodLife Music Ent, a music group that seeks to inspire upcoming artists in Kwekwe.

Music

Latest Release

Ellina, Primiered on Youtube, 21 May 2021

Ellina is a love song that narrates or relates to the common problem young couples facing in their relationships, which is money. True love exists, but the truth is if you don’t have money it’s going to be incredibly difficult to find and keep a woman.

Amai VeVana, Primiered on Youtube, 27 July 2020

Amai Vevana, narrates the beauty of love in all. The track is a soft, poetic romantic song produced by Richboe who did a great job in maintaining the simplicity of the song, filling it with vocals, a simple leading acoustic guitar, and a brass orchestral on the choruses, giving the track a sort of a jazzy feeling to it.
